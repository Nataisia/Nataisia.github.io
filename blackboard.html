Background

Blackboard is a virtual learning environment and learning management system used by educational institutions to facilitate online learning. It provides a platform for instructors to create and manage course content, assignments, assessments, and communication with students. Students can access course materials, submit assignments, participate in discussions, and engage with educational content through Blackboard's online interface.

Problem

Educational platforms are meant to be intuitive and easy to navigate, however, there are some common issues that have been reported. The first being that some users find the interface to not be user-friendly. Navigating course material and finding assignments are challneging for the user. In relation to this, unlike other educational tools, there is no way to track assignment progress in blackboard.

Goal

My goal is to utilize user research from other college students to find the most feasible approach to solving this problem. I want to create a user friendly, and stress free environement for students. Aliong with this, creating additional calendar features that will help organize and keep track of assignments in desktop format.

Reflection

Working as a student remediator at the university of southern california, i have learned a lot about blackboard. One thing most notable about it is that it is not user friendly. I had to reorganize blackboard for a student who was visually impaired, and we found that reorganizing it this way also helps others students without disabilities. At USC, ive heard many people including myself complain about the user interface of blackboard, and so i used the old blackboard to sketch a newer and improved blackboard. The visuals of it are sort of similar to that of canvas, another educational tool.
