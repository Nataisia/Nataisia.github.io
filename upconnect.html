Background

Upconnect is a mentorship app developed to empower students by connecting them with experienced industry professionals in their chosen fields. Created during my New Technologist Internship, the platform serves as a user-friendly interface, streamlined mentor matching, and a supportive community, Upconnect aims to provide valuable insights and guidance for students to advance their careers, creating a collaborative learning experience for the next generation of professionals. In order to make this app successful, my team and I brainstormed, conducted user research interviews, and utilized JavaScript html, and css to create a functioning website for our platform.

User Research

My team and i conducted user research on 4 other college students like ourselves from all from different colleges. It was made sure that each interviewee was not like the other to increase our chances of having more accurate data. We asked each interviewee if theyve every had a mentor before, if they did, we would ask what they liked about it, and what they disliked. Next, we would ask if they have used other platforms that would connect them to other professionals, to see what our strengths and weaknesses were in terms of competition. As a team, we saw this as an opportunity to improve our orginal ideas, and shape it into a product that is more user friendly, and easy to navigate.

What I've Learned

During my time as a new technologist intern, and throughout the process of producing this application, I have learned about the mentorship process as well as the necessary steps that need to be taken in order to match a student and a mentor, for the best possible outcome.
